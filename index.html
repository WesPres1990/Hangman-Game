Psychic Game!

Guess what letter I'm think about. You have ten chances to enter a letter and guess correctly.

If you guess correctly, your "Wins" score goes up; if you guess incorrectly, your "Losses" score goes up.

You will be informed of how many guesses you have left and which letters you have chosen, so as to avoid choosing the same letter more than once.

The game ends when you have run out of chances or have guessed the correct letter, and restarts upon entering a new letter.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the random letter and reset your chances to ten.
